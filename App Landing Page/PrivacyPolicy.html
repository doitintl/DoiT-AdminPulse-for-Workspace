Overview

Thank you for using the Security Checklist for Workspace Admins. This Privacy Policy outlines how your information is handled when using the tool. By using the tool, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Google Sheet Add-on

The Security Checklist for Workspace Admins is a Google Sheets™ Add-on created to share my learning experiences with Appscript, general security awareness, and to help the Google Admins community. When you use these extensions, Google sign-in will authenticate your identity and provide you the option to share certain personal information, such as your email address, only in a few cases.

If acquired, the information you provide is held in strict confidence, as mentioned in this privacy policy. Please also review Google’s privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy to read more about it.

Information Sharing

Your Google sign-in may authenticate your identity and, in some cases, share your email address with us. This information is handled with utmost confidentiality and is used only as described in this privacy policy.

Confidentiality

We treat the information you provide with the highest level of confidentiality. Your data is securely stored and is not shared with any third parties except as outlined in this privacy policy.
